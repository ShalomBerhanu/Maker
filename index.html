About Us

We're a non-profit organization founded with the aim of inspiring others by celebrities' stories. The people we tell the stories of are appropriately chosen from famous people that we all know. These people have one way or another helped others, came up with dynamic ideas, supported the poor community, reached out to unawared people or generally, changed the world.
